NOVAK DJOKOVIC WAS DEPORTED

The final decision was on Djokovic’s case: the Australian Open defending champion, Novak Djokovic was deported, and will not be able to compete. How did we get there?

Since 2010, Novak Djokovic has followed a strict diet cutting gluten, following consultation with nutritionist Dr Igor Cetojevic. And today, we can clearly see the results, as he is currently one of the most dominant athletes in sport, covering the tennis court like a robot. During the month of April 2020, Novak declared he “was opposed to vaccination”, while also stating he was “no expert”.
